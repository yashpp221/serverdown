How We Track Ratings

All ratings are tracked and managed using our custom rating system based on the following criteria:

  • Customer Rating: Based on user satisfaction with delivery speed, package condition, and communication.
  • Seller Rating: Based on business feedback regarding logistics efficiency, pickup times, and overall performance.
  • Feedback: Collected from both customers and sellers, allowing us to improve service quality and address specific concerns.

Your input helps us keep our logistics partners accountable and continuously improving their services.
